With GPT-4

A new exploration of the game level design process.

In-content Learning

We will feed GPT-4 samples like the picture left to design game levels. In the samples, we abstract the level unit into small squares.

We also explain the player's starting point and end point, the types of enemies, the location of the ground and the door, and the player’s possible routes to GPT-4.

Chain of Thought Prompting

The route is usually not single. Players can bypass some enemies, but the routes are usually longer. If a player chooses a shortcut to pass the level, it generally means more difficulty. (Facing three enemies at the same time is a big challenge)

To teach GPT-4 this type of level design theory, we will also input a series of level design concepts and sample design ideas into the model as Chain of Thought Prompting. In this way, GPT-4 can learn the reasoning process of level design.
